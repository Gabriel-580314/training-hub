Science the program is built on
🫸
Stretch under load is the #1 hypertrophy driver. Exercises are picked so the hardest part of the rep is the lengthened position. Kassiano / Maeo / Pedrosa 2023–24
💪
Triceps long head = ~60–65% of upper-arm mass and only fully stretches with the arm overhead — so overhead extensions are non-negotiable, every arm day.
🎯
Lateral delts build shoulder width, not pressing. Trained 2× weekly with cable tension and moderate load through the stretched range.
🦵
Calves are slow-twitch dominant → high frequency (every session) + a 2–3s pause at full stretch, which is the actual growth stimulus.
⏱️
MPS peaks 24–36h, returns to baseline by 48–72h. Lagging muscles get hit 2×/week (calves 5×) to keep protein synthesis elevated.
